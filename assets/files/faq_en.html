FAQ – Millime

Q01: What is Millime?

Millime is a mobile application offered to you by your financial institution.

Millime can be download in blink of an eye from Google Store (IOS) or Google Play (Android).

Millime respects the laws and texts of regulator concerning your personal data and the security of transactions.

Millime offers you a payment account backed by a wallet to carry out your transactions remotely and face-to-face.

Once the application has been downloaded, you will just need your mobile phone and an internet connection to use Millime.

Q02: What is a payment account?

The payment account is an account wording in tunisian dinar used, on an exclusive basis, to perform payment services.

Any natural or legal person can have a payment account on production of the documents required for enrollment.

The payment accounts are of three (3) levels N1, N2 and N3:

  • N1: Balance capped at 500 dinars and cumulative cash outflow capped at 250 dinars per day.

  • N2: Balance capped at 1000 dinars and cumulative cash outflow capped at 500 dinars per day.

  • N3: Balance capped at 5000 dinars and cumulative cash outflow capped at 1000 dinars per day.

Q03: How to become a customer?

Enrollment can be done remotely (e-KYC) or face-to-face (KYC) with a payment agent or in a branch:

  • Remote enrollment for level 1 and 2 accounts.

You need to fill out the registration from, scan – front and back – your valid ID document and take a selfie or streaming video depending on the account level requested.

Your data entered remotely is validated by means of an OTP received from the e-KYC system and forwarded from the smartphone indicated during enrollment.

  • Face-to-face enrollment with retailer payment agent for level 1 account, principal payment agent for level 1 and 2 account and branch for all level account.

You must present your valid ID document to be scanned and complete an enrollment form. The level 2 account require proof of address, and the level 3 account require proof of address and proof of income.

The enrollment process also requires approval of the general conditions of use (GCU), the personal data privacy policy and the signing of an account agreement.

The enrollment process end with the issuance of an authentication SMS or email containing Login and Password to access Millime application.

At the first connection you are required to change this password. You can from this moment fund your account and carry out payment transactions.

Q04: What are the services offered by Millime?

Millime allows you to initiate and/or receive on us and off us payment orders:

  • Funding your account by cash payment (cash in), bank transfer and transfer received, including from a payment card or receipts from abroad,

  • The debt of your account by cash withdrawal (cash out), electronic merchant payment, bill payment, school registrationpayment, phone top-up, bank transfer and transfer issued, direct debit authorised.

Millime is easy to use, available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week and very secure to issue and receive payment orders instantly.

The financial institution provides you with its network of branches and its dense network of payment agents to serve you better.

With Millime you have access to the ATM network as part of interoperability.

The menu of Millime application allows you to discover the services offered and the corresponding pricing, including several services are free like enrollment, cash in account maintenance, etc. and others are at the lowest cost.

Any order initiated by you causes an OTP (One Time Password) to be sent by Millime. You signify your agreement by forwarding the OTP to the payment platform within the time allowed.

As a security measure, all transactions are subject to scheduled checks and prior validation; once confirmed and executed, the order becomes irrevocable.

Q05: Can i make a claim if necessary?

Millime offers you the possibility to formulate claims concerning transactions carried out in the current decade and the financial institution undertakes to process any claim from the Customer and to respond to it, by any means leaving a written record, within one month of its receipt.

Q06: What to do if my phone is lost or stolen?

Millime is an application secured by Login and Password or their substitute PIN (Personal Identification Number).

The user has the possibility to change his password or PIN at any time.

However, in the event of loss or theft of your phone, you can contact us by calling the Hot Line (+21670123456), by email by accessing the contact us section of the website www.millime.com.tn or by contacting one of our branches.

Upon receipt of the claim, your account will be locked until you take action to lift the lock.

Q07: What to do if you change your phone?

With the new smartphone, you install Millime application.

Log in with the same Login and Password or PIN.

Q08: What to do in case of change of mobile phone number?

Millime offers you a service to change your telephone number.

A validation of the new mobile phone number will be requested by OTP.

The new number will be linked to the wallet and the information will be brought to the attention of the mobile switch.

Q09: What to do if you forget the password?

Millime allows you to designate, during enrollment, an email address and/or a mobile phone number for forgotten password recovery by means of an OTP to be approved by you.

Q10: Can i use Millime application if i don’t have internet access?

An active internet connection – Wi-Fi, 3G, 4G, 5G – is absolutely necessary for the use of Millime application.
